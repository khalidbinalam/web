About Me

Hey! I'm Khalid Bin Alam, a passionate audio professional deeply immersed in the world of electronic music. With over 6 years shaping sounds, I wear multiple hats in the industry.

My Creative Aliases:

  • Zalvix

    Focus: Chill House & Electronic Dance Music (EDM).

    Under Zalvix, I explore rhythmic grooves, atmospheric soundscapes, and melodies crafted for both clubs and contemplative listening. It's about finding the balance between energy and emotion.

  • Luminous Lullabies

    Focus: Spiritual Ambient & Relaxing Soundscapes.

    This project is dedicated to creating calming auditory experiences for meditation, focus, sleep, or simply unwinding. Expect ethereal textures and evolving sonic landscapes.

Beyond creating, I'm an experienced Audio Engineer specializing in mixing and mastering, helping artists achieve polished, professional sound. As a DJ, I love curating journeys through diverse electronic genres.

My passion culminated in founding PlayLSD Records, a platform dedicated to discovering and promoting fresh talent in the electronic music scene. My mission is to blend creativity with technical expertise and foster a vibrant community.
