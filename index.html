About Me

I am Khalid Bin Alam, an electronic music producer, playlist curator, and tech entrepreneur hailing from Cumilla, Bangladesh. With a dynamic career spanning over six years, I immerse myself in music production, audio engineering, digital marketing, and web development. My passion lies in redefining the landscape for independent creatives in today's music industry.

As the founder of PlayLSD, I've built more than just a brand—it's a vibrant music discovery platform. My mission is to bridge the gap between emerging underground artists and a global audience of electronic music enthusiasts. I thrive on innovation, authenticity, and fostering a strong sense of community.

My creative expression takes form under two distinct musical aliases:

  • 🎵 Zalvix: Crafting high-energy EDM that combines melodic house, future bass, and festival-grade anthems, designed for both mainstage impact and intimate listening.
  • 🌌 Luminous Lullabies: Exploring the serene realms of ambient and downtempo music, creating atmospheric journeys with organic textures and minimalist soundscapes perfect for meditation and deep focus.

Every track is a personal endeavor – 100% self-produced, mixed, and mastered, reflecting my commitment to sonic precision and genuine artistic vision.

🎧 Artist Submissions & Promotion

Through the PlayLSD submission portal, independent artists can submit their tracks for free. Accepted submissions receive:

  • Cross-platform promotion (blogs, social media, YouTube embeds).
  • Metadata optimization for Spotify algorithm discoverability.
  • Feature spots in artist-focused blog posts and interviews.

Using a tech-first approach, I automate submission management with Spotify API integration, Google Sheets automation, and Apps Script for efficiency and quality.

⚙️ PlayLSD's Web Stack & Tech

Beyond music, I am the technical architect of the PlayLSD digital experience, utilizing:

  • Spotify API, YouTube embeds, and Google Apps Script for automation.
  • HTML, JavaScript, and Blogger for front-end & content.
  • Cloudflare CDN, Hostinger hosting, and custom DNS routing.
  • SEO-friendly architecture, fast-loading UI, and meta-tag optimization.

The playlsd.site platform is a performance-optimized hub for music, blogs, and submissions.
